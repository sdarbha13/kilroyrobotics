What Is It?

Apricot Princess is the second album by Rex Orange County, released on April 26, 2019.

This album, unlike his first, is a very grand work of art. It features an orchestra, and his voice is much more broad and full. Apricot Princess is the nickname O'Conner gave to his loved one. Unlike Bcos U Will Never B Free, Apricot Princess is much more upbeat and satisfying to listen to.

Specifics

Popular track include:

Television- This song features a fluctuating beat and raw vocals to allow the listeners to become immersed in the tempo of this song. O'Conner creates powerful emotions to feel nearly palpable. Furthermore, he describes the complications of his past relationships and confesses feelings for his loved one.

Untitled- O'Conner explains the song himself as he states, “It’s a song all about the feeling you get when you do something you regret and coming to terms with your own choices. I always knew I’d make a video for this song and always had this concept in mind. It’s essentially just a visual representation of what’s being said in the song.”
