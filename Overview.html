An Overview

Raised in a remote village in England, Alexander O' Conner was very passionate about music at a young age. At the age of 17, O' Conner changed his stage name to Rex Orange County and released his debut album, "Bcos u Will Never B Free". His early use of jazz, soul, folk, pop, and indie music allowed him to be a very successful singer/songwriter. In April of 2016, Rex Orange County released his second full-length album, "Apricot Princess". His vivid imagery and beautiful lines attracted talented artists, such as Tyler the Creator. At the age of 20, Rex Orange County has received multiple awards, including BBC's Sound of 2018 award, and he has also featured on the Grammy Award Nominated Album, Flower Boy.
