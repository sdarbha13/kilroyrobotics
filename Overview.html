An Overview

Hello all! Welcome to the Guide, an interactive platform created to help users protect their information. It features easy-to-read descriptions, as well as various diagrams and pictures to aid the descriptions. Also, it has a simple step-by-step process to protect themselves from the most common attacks. Finally, it has dozens of external links to help users better understand the steps and rules to protect one's identity on the internet. The platform's goal is to help everyone protect and understand the value of their information.
